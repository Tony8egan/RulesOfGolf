Other Relief

Dangerous Animal condition


  • The player may take relief at the nearest point of complete relief and receive no penalty
  • For purposes of this Rule, the nearest point of complete relief means the nearest point (not nearer the hole) where the dangerous animal condition does not exist.
  • If playing from a bunker, and the player decides to take relief outside the bunker, the player will incur a one shot penalty.

    • Dangerous Animal condition in Penalty area


  • The player may take relief at the nearest point of complete relief within the penalty area and receive no penalty
  • The player may take relief at the nearest point of complete relief outside the penalty area and receive a one shot penalty
  • For purposes of this Rule, the nearest point of complete relief means the nearest point (not nearer the hole) where the dangerous animal condition does not exist.
