Searching for your ball

Allowed Search Time

A player is given up to 3 minutes to search for their ball after each shot. This time starts from the time that you get to the general area of where your ball landed.


Identifying/Verifying golf ball

In some situations you might find a ball and aren't sure if it is yours. You should make every attempt to identify the ball without touching it.

If there is no way of identifying the ball without touching it, you can mark the balls location and rotate it to identify if it is yours. You must not clean the ball and should return it to it's original position


Accidentally moving ball

If you or your playing partners accidentally move your ball while searching for it, there is no penalty but it should be returned to it's original position.



This information is organized in conjunction with rule 7 of the official rules of golf. For more information on this, see the official rules of golf website here.
