Ball entered water

In order to qualify as having lost a ball in a water hazard, you must be 100% certain that the ball is in the hazard. If you are unsure, you must treat the situation as a lost ball. The procedures for a lost golf ball can be found here.

If you are certain that your ball entered a water hazzard, you must consider the point where your ball entered the water. Look at the markings that surround the water hazzard as these will determine the relief available to you and where it can be taken from.
